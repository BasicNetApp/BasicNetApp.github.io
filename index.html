NoteNet

A new way to take notes on Windows


For faster and easier access the app is accessible from a Bubble ! Click on the icon to bring up the application or on the add button to take a quick note !

The bubble is not intrusive. It remains in the bottom right of the screen and is accessible at all time during your work session, for example.

The bubble can be disabled in the options menu.

Rupee

A simple app to create port forwarding using the UPnP protocol

By default, the protocol used for redirection is UDP and the allotted time is 24 hours.
Of course you can select a custom port between 45000 and 65536
